About Me

As a self-proclaimed nerd and someone who finds solace in making infographics for fun, I am passionate about learning and value knowledge as the ultimate currency.

I am a former student of many disciplines including: business, marketing, real estate, photography, and film, just to name a few. My strong investigative nature and inherent sense of curiosity about the world fueled my desire to pursue higher education in the field of media. In 2016, I graduated from San Diego State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism.

Yet after completing school, I entered the workforce in an entirely unrelated field and have been working as an eCommerce analyst for the past two years. This professional venture allowed me to expand my technical and analytical skill set, and ultimately motivated me to learn more about web development.

I am currently attending the UC San Diego Coding Bootcamp to further expand my knowledge and discover new creative outlets! #foreverstudent
